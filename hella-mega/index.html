[Music] Hella Mega

Kelimutu
2021-09-01
Music
English

I was so happy when I know that Green Day is going to visit Asia for their Hella Mega Tour when it was originally planned in 2020. I bought the ticket for their concert at Singapore (which supposed to be the opening concert for the whole tour), and even arranged the flight and hotel already. But then the pandemic hit so they have to cancel the concert. I don’t know if there’s gonna be another chance for them to go here, but hopefully, I will still be able to see them perform live someday. Finger crossed!

Now they finally able to do the tour, although limited to the US for this year (then Erupe for 2022). They started at Milwaukee, WI, just at the beginning of September this year (yep, today). So, to celebrate the start of the long-awaited Hella Mega Tour from my beloved band, I uploaded a couple of new songs on my YouTube today. Hope you enjoy these!

Good Riddance (Time of Your Life)

If you love Wake Me up When September Ends or Boulevard of Broken Dreams, you might gonna like Good Riddance too. It’s a typical chill accoustic song from Green Day so make sure to check out the original song if you haven’t.

One of My Lies

This one is such a good oldie. One of My Lies is from one of the earliest album they have, Kerplunk!. And oh boy! How I love the lyric. Kerplunk! is probably one of my most favorite album from them, so you should at least check out the original version of this song.
