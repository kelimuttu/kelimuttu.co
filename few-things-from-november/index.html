A few things from November

Kelimuttu.co
2015-12-16
2015
English

I know, I know. It’s been December already. But still, let me sum up what happen in November as my own journal.

  • Start a new month with MozPicnic in Taman Sudirman, Jakarta. It was my very first park experience in Jakarta and I instantly love Tamsud! <3

  • Since I have a lot of time this month, i can do so much things to hone my creativity. Learn to play guitar (I can do 2 songs now, woohoo!), crafting (create 2 books for myself since I’m run out of notebook), and also doodling. It feels so good to do these things after such a long time. (:
  • Survive in Jakarta without gojek or any other online-jek app and I feel proud of myself. Haha :D
  • Attend Tech in Asia Jakarta 2015 which is so crowd yet inspiring.

  • I never been a fan of gigantic mall in Jakarta. I come to GI several times, but only this time I discover the place that feels like home for me. Definitely will come back here.

  • I love this TED talk from Brene Brown about vulnerability. She was indeed, the real story teller. I’m a fan!

  • Experience the worst mental drop this year when Mbah Kung passed away in 16th.
  • Had a dream of duet with GD! OMG, best dream ever in this year! :D
  • Had a bizarre dream the day after dreaming about GD. But then I discover the similar situation in my dream when I recite (Az-Zumar ayat 20) in the afternoon. Whoa, this is real coincident.
  • Craziest thing I done this month : Cut my own hair myself. Sometimes when I’m bored, I can do something crazy just to boost my mood. And thanks god, it works well. I even love my new cut btw. (:
