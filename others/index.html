Like many others

Kelimutu
2017-06-19
Poetry
English

Like many others
I just see without noticing
Talk without listening
Or sometimes listen but ignoring

Like many others
I just do without thinking
I just rant without do something
Just blame without reflecting
Ask without giving

Like many others
I just consume without creating
I just curse without evaluating
I just comment without suggesting
Just write without practicing

And like many others
I solely a slave with nothing
