Monochrome-ish navy

Kelimuttu.co
2017-01-22
Minimalism
English

So, here we are again. Talking about minimalism. Like, finally. :D

Well, I’m not a fashionista myself, but I do care about what I wear. And I want my look to reflect my inner soul. That’s why I always try to pull something that is not only nice on me, but also something that is simple and not trouble me much to mix and match them.

So, I set a goal. And nooo, it is not about reducing. But rather for me to make an automation process in day to day life when it comes to pick my daily outfit. Something that easy to be paired with the rest of the things in my cupboard.

And soo, by many many experience (it takes years like seriously) I finally found my go to colors. Those are black, white, and navy! Black and white are always there from my very first minimalism journey. But navy, oh boy. I used to hate to wear blue cloth. Only after I have a pair of navy t-shirts (that are actually the company uniform from my previous job) that I finally fall for this color.

That is something about navy that I like. I like how it create another shade in my stack of cloths. Because, black and white is feels monotonous often times. I also love how the color doesn’t look too conspicuous.

It’s not easy to find wardrobe recipes that suite our taste, of course. I’m still figuring out my go-to brand too. But at least for now, if you give me 15 minutes in a cloth store, I can probably have my choice is less than 5 minutes. (;
