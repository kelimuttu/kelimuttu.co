Few things on November

Kelimuttu.co
2017-01-24
2016
English

Fear turns the brain off. And how so often when fear takes over, one does the wrong thing. Mitchel Baker

  • Trip to Purwokerto for Riris’s wedding. So glad for her that I spontaneously decide to go to Purwokerto only a day before the wedding.

  • Note to self: When in doubt, don’t choose the easiest option

  • Look who come to Firefox birthday party in Mozilla Space Jakarta!!

  • Finally, visit mbak Peb in her apartment. Talk a lot, eat a lot more. A nice brain-less day. :D

  • Most importantly, those who become succesful initiate. They reach out to people, ask questions, make recommendations, offer to help, and pitch their ideas.

  • Hipster tech meetup was awesome!

  • Tech in Asia.

  • I learn something from Karl Dubost, Mozilla’s web compatibility engineer that I’ve met in Tech in Asia 2016 and during his class in Mozilla space Jakarta. It’s not impossible to live our life to the fullest even without some today’s common technologies that we think we need.

  • You know when others perceive you as a strong person, they usually ask you to sacrifice. Because you know, YOU ARE THAT STRONG. Or if I may say, because you’re so good on make it up as like, you don’t bother. And I hate it sometimes. I’m sick of people pointing out to me to step back and sacrifice. I’d love to make my choice on my own will.
  • I’m getting MozSpace sleepover more and more lately.
  • One by one goes back to Semarang this month. I feel like there is less and less reason to stay.
  • Renang sama anak2 kos. Seruuuu!
  • I was trapped to watch All about Eve & Casablanca (These were classic aka super old movies). But it turns out, that those were still enjoyable and I kinda like it. Even if, it felt awkward initially.
