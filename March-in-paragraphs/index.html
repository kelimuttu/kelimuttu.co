March in paragraphs

Kelimutu
2017-05-14
2017
English

Spend money because it’s an investment in your own well-being. Not because you’re bored and have nothing else to do. - Sophia Amoruso

  • March is all about reconnect. It feels good to catching up on each other’s progress & issues.
  • Finally had a chance to meet my uni bestfriend. I feel refreshed, like someone pushed a reset button on my head.
  • I bought a camera! I’ve been wanting to learn photography since forever. And to remember that I have some travel plans in this year, I was thinking to buy a camera as my travel companion. And so my choice finally fell on Fujifilm X-A10 to consider that I’m just starting out and it comes as a package with the polaroid camera. ^^
  • Spend the Bali’s day of silence (or Nyepi day) in Ubud and the rest of the month island hopping in Bali, Lombok, and Gili Air.
  • Found a startup which share the same idea with my final project in university (which is social media for community). I feel like someone has finally realizing my dream.
  • Movie of the month: Nocturnal animals. I’m super amazed by how clever is the way Tom Ford telling us a story of a revenge.

  • Realization on Bali’s day of silence:

    It really doesn’t matter even if you don’t believe in yourself. And nobody does. So you shouldn’t worry much about it. I mean, it’s a natural process to question yourself. And I actually should be thankful because that means that I care about my life. I naturally care about my intentions in this planet.

I think those people who are jealous by someone else’s fortune because they’re thinking that they deserve it more are the worst people.

  • Didn’t cook so much this month cause I spent the month mostly in Tegal and travelling.
