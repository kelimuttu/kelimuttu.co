Few things in a half of February

Kelimuttu.co
2016-02-13
2016
English

Everything now is feels more normal. Unlike the first couple of weeks when I first moved to Jakarta. Now I got more familiar with the blaring sounds of the queued cars in the crossroads (eventho’ I still hate it). The hustle of the office? Uhm, I’m closer to get used to it now. The major change? It would be that I eat more regularly than ever since I always surrounded by people that I can ask to eat together (Yay!).

But the thing is, now I’m lacking of time to spent with my own self. But then apparently, I get to do that this weekend. So, before my mood is shrinking out, let’s wrap it up now.

Spend every friday in the past 2 weeks for karaoke. The first one is with Mozilla gank + Konstantina. And the second is with Gnews team. I’m just too weak to refuse a karaoke offer. :b

Had a meetup with ante Febri, om Momo, and Ongse earlier this month. Ante Pebi used to be the person who like to mocked me for having a bakpao cheek. Now after 5 months, she finally know the feeling of having her own bakpao cheek. :b

  • Never underestimate the power of sharing your dream. You probably don’t know how close your dreams are, until someone told you so.
  • Watch Interstellar and also, The martians this week. Which then ignite my long time obsession over space life.
  • Stumble upon this article when I played around with Gnews in “Minimalism”keyword - The art of self-possession. A great reminder that we have to take some times to take care of our inner-soul. Just like me, having this kind of me time to write this article.
  • Discover this article today. I feel like I need to bookmark that one to remind me that even if the space life is cool, the earth will always be the most beautiful one.
