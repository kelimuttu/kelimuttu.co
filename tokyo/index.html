[music] Tokyo - Yui

Kelimutu
2018-12-19
Music
English


I’ve been listening to Yui since high school and when I know that this song has a pretty easy chord, I decided to give it a try and make a cover.

I should note that I change the tuning of my guitalele to the common guitar standard (not Guitalele standard anymore). I think it’s been awhile since I use Guitalele tuning and it confuse me sometimes when I use a normal guitar (both has a slightly different chord). I need to re-rearn some chords now that I’m back to the normal tuning.

Also, I don’t speak Japanese so I’m sure there might be some spelling mistakes in the video, but overall I’m pretty satisfied with the final result. And in addition, I made the acoustic version just recently. (:
