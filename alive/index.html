Alive

Kelimuttu.co
2016-10-16
Jar of thoughts
English

I don’t know how many readers that may probably notice that I’ve been away without a single post in this blog for the past 2 months. I knoooow, I’m a bad blogger. But look. I’m back here & I’m still alive. :D

Everything is safe and sound. Even though October is the heaviest month for me so far. Some changes are around the corner. I should say that it’s actually sad and relieving at the same time. It’s sad because hey, who loves change? We all love the constant feeling of safety, don’t we? But then again, I have reached the limit where I feel extremely tired of my routines. And that is actually the part that relieve me. I will have more time to rest with this change. At least, there will no more busy-Kiki until the end of this year. (:

Buuuut apart of anything happen in my life, I love the weather of this month. The chilly air of October this year somehow remind me of Europe. But of course, here is better because it’s not as cold as London’s summer. Even though, the rain often keep me worry if it causing flood around my neighborhood. You knooow, Jakarta thing.

Anyway, I’m so happy lately to receive some more feedback about minimalism living that I’m currently trying. I actually have some topics about minimalism in mind. But let’s wait until I have more time to write, okay? For now, let’s just end this post with an introductory photo of my favourite stuff lately. “Spot!!”

Spot & Pudee. My weekend buddies.
